About Us

Learn more about our church and mission

Our Story

Eldopoly Cu Union was founded in 1995 with a small group of believers who gathered to worship and study God's Word. Over the years, we've grown into a vibrant community of faith, serving our local area and beyond.

Our mission is to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in their spiritual growth, and serve our community with love and compassion.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit in the lives of believers.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and make disciples.
